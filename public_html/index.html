Shells And Tails

Personal Project

Shells and Tails is a competitive split-screen party game where a rabbit and turtle race to win control of the farm. Players choose their character and take turns selecting mini-games, determined by a coin flip. The game offers a mix of exciting mini-games, from platforming challenges to stealth missions, making each match a test of different skills. It's best of three, and each time a player loses, they get to choose the next mini-game.

Mini-Games:
  • Speedrunner: Race to the finish while avoiding obstacles and navigating platforms.
  • Dinner_Time: Collect vegetables and cross a road while dodging waves of farm animals.
  • Metal_Gear: Sneak through a dark cave without getting caught by wolves, or be sent back to the previous checkpoint.
  • Skyfall: Stay on collapsing platforms and be the last one standing.
  • Treasure_Hunt: Explore a large map to find three hidden coins and claim victory.
  • Party_Animals: Guess how many animals crossed the screen in a timed challenge.
Key Contributions:
  • Developed the core mechanics for the mini-games, including platforming, stealth, and puzzle elements.
  • Designed and implemented the split-screen functionality, allowing players to use gamepads or keyboards.
  • Integrated a coin flip system to decide which player selects the first mini-game, enhancing competitive balance.
  • Built a system that allows the losing player to choose the next mini-game, keeping gameplay engaging and dynamic
  • Implemented keybinding features, ensuring flexible controls for different players.
